Providing Quality Dental Care in Palm Springs and Cathedral City, CA

Our goal at Precision Dentistry is to provide exceptional, comprehensive dental care to our patients in a friendly and caring environment, listen to your concerns, educate you on your dental health, and help you decide on the best dentistry plan for you and your family. We are devoted to restoring and enhancing the natural beauty of your smile using conservative, state-of-the-art procedures that will result in beautiful, long lasting smiles!

We provide various procedures including cosmetic dentistry, veneers, full mouth reconstruction, implants, periodontal maintenance, and oral surgery. As dental professionals we are committed to continuing our education and sharing our knowledge with each other and our patients. We strive to work together as a team and to respect every persons individuality. We are a group of kind and compassionate professionals, who enjoy making your dental office visit the kind of experience you've always wished it would be!

Our Dental Procedures

Cosmetic Dentistry

We'll help you improve the appearance of your smile through one or more of our cosmetic dentistry procedures, such as teeth whitening, veneers, and more.

Gum Disease/Periodontics

The build up of plaque and target can result in gum disease, aka "periodontal disease" is a bacterial infection of the gums and tissues surrounding and supporting your teeth.

Filings

To restore and repair the surface of a tooth, we offer a variety of dental fillings for teeth that have been damaged by decay, fracture or wear.

Dental Cleanings

To maintain good oral hygiene, routine dental checkups are a must. Save time, discomfort and money with early detection.

Crowns

A Dental crown fully restores a tooth's shape, strength and size, encasing the visible portion of your tooth or dental implant.

Wisdom Teeth & Oral Surgery

Wisdom Teeth, sometimes referred to as "third molars" are the last to erupt. We are oral surgery and wisdom teeth removals through speciality services.
