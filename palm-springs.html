Meet Our Palm Springs Dental Team

Rose - Front Office Manager

Rose has been in the dental field since 1977 as a dental assistant.  Over the years she has consistently provided the highest level of service to every patient, and continues to do so.  She will greet you with a welcoming smile and treat you like a family member of her own family, making your visit a pleasant experience.  She is actively involved in the community, including the Palm Springs Chamber of Commerce and Hospitality Association, helping spread the word about the importance of proper dental care.Rose has 2 children, 2 grandchildren and spends her spare time with her husband Scott and fur "kids" Moki, Gunner, Trixie, Pua, and Ku'uipo and exploring all that this desert paradise has to offer!

Danielle - RDH - Registered Dental Hygienist

After growing up in Wisconsin and practicing as a Registered Dental Hygienist for over 12 years, her and her husband relocated to the Coachella Valley in the spring of 2019.  They enjoy the desert climate, its benefits to our health, and have made their permanent home with their fur babies.  She takes pride in providing her patients thorough dental cleanings with a gentle touch as well as sharing useful oral home care tips.  When not in the office, she enjoys traveling to other warm weather destinations, attending live music events, walking the dogs, and cheering on the Green Bay Packers (always a cheesehead at heart).  She is excited and very blessed to be part of the kind and professional team at Precision Dentistry.... She looks forward to caring for your smile.  Danielle will be working at both offices, Palm Springs and Cathedral City.
